About Us

Our Mission: To drive financial inclusion and empower African SMEs and farmers through blockchain technology

Transparency Inclusion Empowerment

At the Walor, we are guided by the principles of Transparency, Inclusion, and Empowerment. We believe that a transparent and accountable approach fosters trust and integrity in our operations. We are dedicated to including all members of the community, regardless of their background or circumstances, ensuring that no one is left behind. Our ultimate goal is to empower individuals and communities, allowing them to take control of their financial futures, and to create a world where financial opportunities are accessible to all.

In our mission to foster inclusion and empowerment, we provide an opportunity for farmers, farmer groups, and SMEs to better their credit scores and increase their chances of accessing financial services. We empower individuals and communities by facilitating the establishment of strong financial credit scores, paving the way for enhanced access to credit through our peer-to-peer lending platform.

This collaborative effort not only benefits our users but also offers actionable data to partnering banks. Banks can utilize this data to make informed decisions and tailor financial services to the needs of these groups. Through blockchain-powered smart contracts, we track and execute loan transactions for farmer groups efficiently and transparently. This innovative approach reduces friction, enhances trust, and makes financial services more accessible, reinforcing our commitment to financial inclusion and the empowerment of African communities.
